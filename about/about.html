  • Hi! My name is Jihee Kim (김지희)
 

I am currently a first year architecture student at Carnegie Mellon University, anticipating a B.Arch in May of 2021.

I am an international student from South Korea.

My interests are in urban design, sustainability and sound design, and my sources of inspiration are art, the nature, traveling, and people. I am intrigued by architecture’s role as an active foundation to people’s interactions and experiences.

Outside of architecture, I enjoy drawing, painting, traveling, singing and writing.
