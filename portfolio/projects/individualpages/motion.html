  • MOTION: PROPERTIES OF TOOLS
  • Spring 2016
 

For this project, each student was assigned a tool. I had to work with a hack saw.

We first analyzed the tool as an object and focused on its geometries.

Then we analyzed the motion of the object in relationship to the user.

The last component of the project was a group project of building a wood model based on sectionl drawing of my tool and my partner's tool, focusing on the "verbs." Through our model, we tried to show the connection between 'oscillate' and 'twist.'
