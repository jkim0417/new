  • CUBE HOUSE: WEDDING PAVILLION
  • Fall 2016
 

First Studio project in the school of architecture. Students were to create a wedding pavillion in the shape of a cube by lake Erie.

The Cube House Project Introduced important concepts of occupancy, spatial relationships, human scale, and the design process.

The underlying theme of my project is unity. The cube dominates the space, the circulation, and occupancy.

In its central position, the cube divides the site into a grid, or different zones, connecting the different spaces that are implied.
